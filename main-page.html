Why Learn About Plastics?

Plastics are everywhere in our daily lives, from the bottles we drink from to the electronic devices we use. Understanding different types of plastics and their properties is crucial for making informed decisions about recycling, sustainability, and material selection in various applications.

Each plastic type has unique characteristics that make it suitable for specific uses. By learning to identify these materials and their properties, we can better understand their environmental impact and make more sustainable choices.

Key Learning Objectives:

  • Identify common plastic types and their recycling codes
  • Understand the unique properties of each plastic material
  • Learn about appropriate applications for different plastics
  • Develop awareness of recycling and sustainability considerations
